About Me

I have had a love of computers since the days of playing barely functioning games on the family windows 95' computer. Eventually I would dabble in video editing and animation after getting my own laptop in highschool.

I attended Savannah College of Art and Design to study animation, but eventually changed my major to interactive design and game development. I graduated with a BFA in 2013 but did not persue a career in the game industry.

During my time in school I took some basic classes on web development and programming, and now I seek to expand my understanding of these skills to hopefully make a career out of it in the days ahead.
